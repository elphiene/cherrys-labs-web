non-steam-grid-generator

overview

A browser-based tool for generating correctly sized Steam artwork for non-Steam games. Automatically produces grid, hero, logo, and icon images at the right dimensions.

tech stack

Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript — no frameworks or build tools. Runs entirely in the browser.
